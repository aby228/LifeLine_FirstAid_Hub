Our Mission

LifeLine First Aid Hub bridges the critical gap between medical emergencies and professional help by delivering accessible, step-by-step emergency medical guidance to anyone, anywhere. Originally Novata conceived to serve rural communities in developing countries where healthcare access is limited, this project has evolved into a comprehensive platform that welcomes learners from all backgrounds‚Äîstudents, educators, healthcare professionals, and community volunteers alike. The website serves as both an educational resource and the central hub for our upcoming mobile application, featuring interactive first aid tutorials, project updates, and community feedback channels. Whether you're a researcher seeking reliable medical protocols or someone who simply wants to be prepared for life's unexpected moments, LifeLine provides the tools and knowledge needed to respond confidently when every second counts.

Timeline

Emergency Response Timeline

This guide breaks down the crucial first 20 minutes of emergency response into five color-coded phases, from immediate assessment to professional medical handover. Each phase shows specific timing and actions, helping responders understand how individual first aid techniques fit together in a coordinated, time-sensitive response plan.

Treatment Decision Flowchart

This flowchart guides first aid responders through critical decision points, from checking consciousness to determining injury severity and appropriate treatment responses. The color-coded pathways help users quickly navigate complex emergency situations and confidently decide when to apply first aid versus when to immediately call for professional help.
